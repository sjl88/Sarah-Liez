Potential Project

I completed an internship this summer with the Factory Farming Awareness Coalition (FFAC) and would like to use what I learned in that experience to educate others on the problematic nature of the industry. This issue is important for a number of reasons, including its impact on the environment, on minority communities, and on animals themselves. This subject matter must be approached delicately while still being clear in stating the facts of the issue, so I am looking forward to figuring out how I would like to frame this issue based on my intended audience.
